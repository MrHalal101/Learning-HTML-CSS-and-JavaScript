Greek Yogurt Protein Bowl

Back to the Recipe Hub

The "Quick & Healthy" Option

Prep Time: 5 mins | Servings: 1 | Dietary: Vegetarian, Gluten-Free

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt (full fat or 2% for creaminess)
  • 1/4 cup crunchy granola
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries or sliced strawberries
  • 1 tsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup

Step by Step:

  1. Scoop the yogurt into a bowl.
  2. Sprinkled the crumbled granola, chia seeds and berries ontop of the yogurt.
  3. Finish with a swirl of honey or maple syrup
