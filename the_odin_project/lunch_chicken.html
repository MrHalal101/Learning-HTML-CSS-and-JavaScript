Pesto Chicken Salad Croissant

Back to the Recipe Hub

The "Classy but Simple" Deli Lunch

Prep Time: 8 mins | Servings: 2 | Dietary: High Protein

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp basil pesto
  • 2 large croissants
  • Optional: Sliced tomato or cucumber

Step by Step:

  1. Mix: In a bowl, combine the shredded chicken, mayo, and pesto until creamy.
  2. Slice: Cut the croissants in half lengthwise.
  3. Fill: Scoop the chicken salad onto the bottom half of the croissant.
  4. Close: Add the optional tomato slices and the top of the croissant.
