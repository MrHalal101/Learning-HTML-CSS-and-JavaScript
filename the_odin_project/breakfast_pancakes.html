The "Weekend Comfort" Classic

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins | Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • Honey
