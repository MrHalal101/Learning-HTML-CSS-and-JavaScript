Overview


Descendents: Affects all in the div. Can be thought of as Children and Grandchildren


Child: Affects only the elements in the First div. Only the Children but NOT the Grandchildren


General Siblings: Affects outside the div which are NOT in another div. Can be thought of as Siblings not Neices/Nephews


Adjecent Siblings: Affects Next Element which is NOT in another div. Can be thought of as the Closest Siblings
