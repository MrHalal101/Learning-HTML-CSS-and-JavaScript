Meal-Prep Burrito Bowls

Back to the Recipe Hub

The "Batch Cooking" Specialist

Prep Time: 15 mins | Servings: 4 | Dietary: Vegetarian

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked quinoa or cilantro-lime rice
  • 2 cans (15oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup frozen corn, thawed
  • 1 cup fresh salsa
  • 4 tbsp Greek yogurt

Step by Step:

  1. Prep: Line up four airtight containers.
  2. Divide: Evenly distribute the quinoa and black beans among the containers.
  3. Toppings: Add a layer of corn and a scoop of salsa to each.
  4. Store: Seal and refrigerate for 3-5 days.
  5. Final Touch: Do not add the yogurt or avocado until the day you eat it to keep it fresh.
