Creamy Tomato One-Pot Pasta

Back to the Recipe Hub

The "Minimal Cleanup" Lunch

Prep Time: 8 mins | Servings: 2 | Dietary: High Protein

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz penne pasta
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1.5 cups water or vegetable broth
  • Grated Cheese
  • Handful of fresh baby spinach

Step by Step:

  1. Simmer: In a small pot, combine pasta, marinara, and water. Bring to a boil.
  2. Absorb: Reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 10-12 minutes until pasta is tender and liquid is mostly absorbed.
  3. Cream: Stir in the cream/cream cheese until the sauce is smooth and orange.
  4. Wilt: Add the spinach and stir for 30 seconds until it wilts into the hot sauce.
